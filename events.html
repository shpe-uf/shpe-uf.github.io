What we're doing

PartnerSHPE

PartnerSHPE is a program to help engineering students at Santa Fe College gain the skills and knowledge to successfully transfer to a university, find internships, and develop their technical careers. Through the semester we offer professional and technical workshops to give students as many resources as possible Join our Facebook group to learn about our events.

Goals For Tomorrow

Come join us as we host our 13th annual soccer tournament to raise scholarship funds for first year hispanic students. There will be lots of great food, music, great company, and the most intense soccer match of the semester!

SHPE Jr.

SHPE Jr. aims to apply the SHPE mission before students have entered college and are still in the early stages of deciding their careers. By doing so SHPE Jr. provides STEM awareness and access to students that might not have had it otherwise. It is the mission of SHPE Jr. to inspire and develop the next generation of engineers that will continue to expand the Hispanic communities' influence in this field.
